HOMYCOCO Amazon Ads CLI Privacy Notice

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Overview

HOMYCOCO Amazon Ads CLI is an internal tool used only by the account owner to access, analyze, and manage our own Amazon Ads campaigns. It is not offered as a public service or third-party advertising platform.

Data We Access

With authorization from the Amazon Ads account owner, the tool may access Amazon Ads account data, including campaigns, ad groups, targeting clauses, bids, budgets, advertising reports, spend, impressions, clicks, orders, sales metrics, and related campaign performance data.

How We Use Data

Data is used only for campaign analysis, readback verification, reporting, and authorized campaign management actions for our own Amazon Ads account.

Storage and Security

OAuth credentials, refresh tokens, reports, and generated execution records are stored only in the account owner's local environment or private project workspace. We do not intentionally publish credentials, tokens, cookies, or sensitive request headers.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, transfer, or share Amazon Ads data with third parties. Data is not used for cross-account profiling or external advertising services.

Retention

Local reports and execution records may be retained for audit, troubleshooting, and campaign performance review. The account owner may delete locally stored files and revoke Amazon authorization at any time.

Contact

For questions about this privacy notice, contact the repository owner through GitHub Issues: https://github.com/IMPLannister/homycoco-privacy/issues.
